About Us

DevSecOps OnTheGo is the brainchild of two passionate individuals, Nitish Deshpande and Aditi Kulkarni, who are committed to making security accessible to developers of all levels. Our mission is to democratize DevSecOps by providing an easy-to-use, Docker-based solution that automates key security practices, such as Software Composition Analysis (SCA), Static Application Security Testing (SAST), and Dynamic Application Security Testing (DAST), for every developer.

Nitish Deshpande, the lead behind DevSecOps OnTheGo, brings years of experience in cybersecurity and development. He envisions a world where developers—whether solo creators or part of a small team—can integrate robust security into their workflows without complex configurations or specialized knowledge.

Aditi Kulkarni, the co-contributor, brings a wealth of expertise in DevSecOps practices and a deep understanding of secure development cycles. Together, they aim to provide a seamless and automated solution that enables developers to shift left and build secure applications right from the start.

DevSecOps OnTheGo is a result of their collective vision to bridge the gap between security and development, empowering every developer to build secure, production-ready code with ease.
